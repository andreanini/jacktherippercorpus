July 18th 1889
Races
Leicester

Dear Boss

I write you a few lines hoping you are geting on nicely they call me an American butcher now. never mind I have started [`operaiton' — deleted] killing again its grand work I dont like give it up just now. I had not time to finish the last one. I shall be in London again to night. Do they think they got a clue they perhaps would like to have. they is another one I have got my eye on her I will close this letter to Dear Boss I Remain

Jack the Ripper
