Sirs, I am sorry that such a letter should be sent to you, viz, the one signed Jack the Ripper . . . I can assure you it is nothing to do with us whatever . . . I caution persons playing such tricks ... to beware of their Bodies for `it's' no joke . . .

I am yours truly.

Mr Englishman

Colchester Oct. 13. 1888.

1213. Shall not return to the E. end trust Sir C. Warren to make such a fuss . . . . . . . . .
