Grovenor Hotel

Dear Boss

I must tell you that I am still looking out for the young girls of Commercial Road. I am giving you a rest for a few months and will go from the Hotel on Saturday morning and I am going to my house in the East India Docks Road the number I will not inform you But I have my eye on one or two more if all goes well I must ask you to inform the police that I will run their fat lean for them, I keep my ... instrument pretty sharp My Bang Bangs are always Loaded redey fore the first Insulter.

I remain a true and

faithfull servant

John or Jack the Ripper

London

good night

Grovenor Hotel
