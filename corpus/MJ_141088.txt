13th of Oct

Sirs

Do you think that you can catch me and my pals if so say it at once and when you have said what you have to say why then we can lofe about, had to jump up just now, thought you had, next time I do any thing it will be at the town in which I write this letter I remain yours murderyfied J.J. Thompson alias J. the Rippers Nephewx

PS Excuse bad writing J.J.T.
