Manchester

Nov. 22nd 88

Dear Boss

I am staying in Manchester at present but dressed as poor man a navvie. saw the paper and you have had a letter from someone signed J. Ripper from Portsmouth soon after I did the last job (Kelly) i have been in Manchester since. Had nothing to do since kelly but seen Jervis's girls [?] every day will visit London about Sunday ready to be in time again on Monday. when i will do
another i will tell you all when i get copped. Good Bye old fellow till i return.

Yours Truly

Jack the Ripper.
