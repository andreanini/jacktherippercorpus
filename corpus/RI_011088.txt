London E.

Monday.

I am so pleased to see another chance of ripping up a dear creature. I shall be in Buck's Row or very close to between 12 & 2 in morning on Wednesday I have got/ the girl set & as I have been offered dooble money for her woomb and lower part of body mean to have them at any price I do like to find them nice parts. I do pity Leather Apron. I've got some/ 
one to write this for me.

yours truly

Boss

Ripper
