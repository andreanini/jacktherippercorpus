Bolton Oct 4th 1888
Dear Gentlemen. I have got to Bolton, and I have got a scent of another Girl. of mine that has been at London and I shall do it for her. i have one to do in Bolton — and one to finish in Burnley Lancashire before Oct 14th and I will give myself up at Manchester this part of country is slow and innocent I am getting sick so I will not be long before I have finished my work
Yours Jack the ripper/
I have written this in a hurry I have not got a stamp, its to late but I shall stay at Bolton until I accomplish my work
and then 20 to Burnley
Jack the Ripper
I shall give myself up on Oct 14th
