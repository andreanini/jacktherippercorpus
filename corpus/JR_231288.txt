Dear Boss

I am on leave now I will be all till after Xmass don't trouble London for me I want my holidays. I must travel all Ireland and Scotland the Police here are not of much use I have been happy to say I have been traveling with some of your Dectives which I met on the 15 hollihead we was together as far as dublin when we parted on Sunday morning of the 16th
