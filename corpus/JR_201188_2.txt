Dear Boss 2[?]4/11/1888

I hope you are quite well as It lives me at present for I am going to commited another murder on Wenesnday just as bad as the other. look out for me for I am going to her head off and her legs off. In fashion street I will be there of all day long. Plese will you pass this to sir Charlie dear Charlie.

Yours truly

Jack the Ripper

I was round the euston when the law [?] took a man on suspion. Don't forget I wrote when very quick becaus I had to catch the trian for queens park
