Wensday.
Dear Boss

I thought I would let you now jest to se if you could be clever enouft to stop me as I [me]an to give you a turn I mean to comense busenis before the weak is out I have spotted one or two near kingston station as I was down there last night minppouse [?] the slops have got [missing] in there eyes some has They have up the old spo[missing] but if they intefier with [m]e look out I got plenty of amineton & my long sticker is bleeding sharp I se by the papers you have yet 2 nice old strewbury nose top on the bench what price last one up the smock so good 

your old pal

Jack the Ripper or
look out to night
