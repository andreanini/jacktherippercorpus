Johney ripper Whitechapel London Road

I shall kill seven more wommon be the 23 of march next and shall kill 7 plocemen in may and for soldiers gone so be on the watch [printed crown and 'Regd. 26 89 31495'] and cut of their tools eat the bugger so remember me to all the old plocemen I have treated four in edgeway road in last December Tseventh O good by old pil I might have luck enough to see you soon I hope and I shall give my self in the month of july beware. jack ripper and son with best love good bye old chums.
