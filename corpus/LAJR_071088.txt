Saturday 7/8/8/8

Dear Old Boss

You see I have some Black blood instead of Red [ ] know I did not have a chance to do what I intended on Saturday night Else there would have been another horrible murder in the East End I will give my self up 8 o'clock @ Peckham Station
Monday I am wearing a lights'uit With love to all

{Leather Apron}
{Jack the Ripper}
