Nov. 11th

[Crude drawing of skull and crossbones]

Sirs - this time
I am not afraid of letting you know the whereabouts I am I can't help but laugh at idea of Sir C. Warren & his blood hounds It is of no use the police to be so reticent in the matter next time head clean off I have my eye on the next on the list there is plenty in Hull all good blood
I have lost the real stuff. Take warning next time I carrie the head away with me in my bag the blood wont [illegible]? I take good care of the uterus. I will give next one gip [?] no mersey.

Jack the R-
