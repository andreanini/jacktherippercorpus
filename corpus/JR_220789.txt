Angust Hotel
Nr B Street
City
July 22nd 1889

Dear Boss

I am about to do another murder tomorrow night or Wednesday night I cant keep from my work long for I love it to much, catch me if you can. this is the last one this side of the water, the next Im going to give the other side a turn, I had a bit of a job with the last one. I had to leave it before I could finish

signed
Jack the Ripper
