['October 27.' Deleted] Nov. 3rd 1888

To Mr Charles warren head Police Scotland yard detectivs.

I. hame pleased to year that I was caught at neath on saturday night at the falcon hotel old market st but you have not had the pleasure of catching me yet by the time you have had this one or two more will feel my knife I have been helpin the police in the seaorch with blood - hounds and rejoices in hearing them say they were on the right track J. T. Ripper

Ah! ah. over next one I copp I'll send the toes and earoles to you for supper

J. T. Ripper

[Drawing of hand holding knife]
